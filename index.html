Arial Beel is the third largest wetland in Bangladesh covering an area of 136 sq. km. It is situated in Sreenagar Upazila of Munshiganj District.

About

Leaving the tumultuous climate of the capital, on the off chance that anyone wishes to breathe easy in the midst of the nature, paying attention to peeping of birds and feeling the calming cool wind blowing over a tremendous waterbody, there is no option in contrast to Arial Beel at Sreenagar in Munshiganj. The 136 square kilometers wetland is arranged among Padma and Dhaleshwari Waterway in the south of Dhaka. The idea of the beel has not changed over the course of the century; it stays with its one of a kind variety. It is contributing perpetually to the neighborhood economy as many individuals acquire their vocation from the beel through fishing just as through cultivating when water retreats throughout the colder time of year..... Outstanding, around 200 types of fish is accessible in the immense wetland that produces a lot of riceand grasses for cows are created in the wetland.The diverse sort of fishes of the wetland are exceptional in taste.
